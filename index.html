about me

Hi there! I'm Renny and I'm a front-end web developer.

I live in the PNW with my husband, two-kids and my in-laws. I've had an interesting
pre-tech life, but a now-infamous pandemic has given me time to delve into something
new. I've been following Skillcrush for years, and the downtime I have had has given
me the time I need to focus on it.

I was immediately hooked after going through the free coding bootcamp and wanted to
learn all the things immediately. That curiosity has opened a door that I'm certain
will never be shut. Learning how to build my first website, I've realized I've found
something that gives me the freedom & versatility I've been looking for in my work and
home life. Now I've made the full switch to front-end development, where I can use my
organizational skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.

I can't wait to see what we can
build together!
